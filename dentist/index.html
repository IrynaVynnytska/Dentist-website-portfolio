Our Services

Dental Cleaning

$75 - $200

A dental cleaning, also known as prophylaxis, involves the removal of plaque, tartar, and stains from your teeth. The dentist or dental hygienist will use special tools to clean your teeth, followed by polishing to make them smooth and shiny. This routine preventive procedure helps maintain oral hygiene and prevents gum disease.

Dental Fillin

$150 - $400

Dental fillings are used to repair teeth that have been damaged by decay or cavities. The dentist will remove the decayed portion of the tooth and fill the space with a suitable material, such as composite resin, amalgam, or porcelain. Fillings restore the tooth's structure, prevent further decay, and improve its function.

Teeth Whitenin

$300 - $800

Teeth whitening, also known as teeth bleaching, is a cosmetic procedure that lightens the color of your teeth, removing stains and discoloration. The dentist may use a bleaching gel and a specialized light source to activate the gel, resulting in brighter and whiter teeth. This procedure helps enhance your smile and boost your confidence.

Dental Crow

$800 - $1,500

A dental crown is a cap-like restoration that covers a damaged or decayed tooth entirely. It is custom-made to match the shape, size, and color of your natural teeth. Crowns are used to restore the strength, functionality, and appearance of a tooth that has undergone extensive decay, undergone a root canal, or has been fractured. They provide protection and improve the tooth's aesthetics.

FAQs

Do you accept dental insurance, and how does the billing process work?

Yes, we accept dental insurance from most major providers. We recommend contacting our dental clinic with your insurance information before your appointment, so we can verify your coverage and provide you with accurate cost estimates. Our administrative staff will assist you in understanding your insurance benefits and handle the billing process directly with your insurance company, making the process as convenient as possible for you.

What are the payment options?

The cost of dental procedures can vary depending on the specific treatment and individual patient needs. We provide transparent and detailed cost estimates for each procedure after evaluating your oral health. We offer various payment options, including cash, credit/debit cards, and dental insurance. Additionally, we may have financing options or installment plans available to make dental care more affordable for our patients.

What qualifications and experience do your dentists have?

Our dental clinic employs highly qualified and experienced dentists. They have completed rigorous education and training, including dental school and relevant certifications. Our dentists stay up-to-date with the latest advancements in dentistry through continuing education courses and professional development. Rest assured, you will receive expert care from our skilled dental professionals.

How often should I visit the dentist for regular check-ups?

It is generally recommended to visit the dentist for regular check-ups every six months. These routine visits allow the dentist to assess your oral health, detect any potential issues early on, and provide professional dental cleanings to maintain your oral hygiene.
